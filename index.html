The Story
Behind the Engineer

I'm Yogesh Kavhar, a First Year Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering student with a deep fascination for how electronic systems and communication networks work. From tinkering with circuits as a kid to exploring the fundamentals of signals and embedded systems, engineering has always felt like my natural calling.

I'm passionate about applying electronics principles to real-world problems — whether it's designing circuits, programming microcontrollers, or understanding wireless communication systems. My goal is to become a skilled ENTC engineer who bridges hardware, software, and communication technologies.

Outside academics, I enjoy experimenting with Arduino projects, exploring electronics concepts independently, and staying updated on developments in IoT, embedded systems, and wireless communication.
